# Amol Shaila Suresh – Research Portfolio
Hi!
I am Amol. I am a development researcher and Chevening Scholar, currently pursuing an MSc in Social Research Methods at the London School of Economics and Political Science. With a strong foundation in development economics and extensive field experience in rural India, I specialize in experimental research design, survey methodology, and econometric analysis. I have led large-scale randomized controlled trials, managed complex datasets, and contributed to securing research funding through proposal development. My work focuses on gender, labour markets, and local governance, with a growing emphasis on mixed-methods research, including qualitative and computational approaches. I bring a combination of rigorous analytical skills, field implementation experience, and project management expertise, and am motivated to contribute to evidence-based policymaking in poverty alleviation and social protection.

This repository hosts my research portfolio as an applied social researcher. It includes selected projects, survey instruments, code snippets, and writing samples from impact evaluation and development policy work.
